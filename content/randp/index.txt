text: RandP is a program for generating chance performances, written in Rust and distributed on Cargo.

Just as figures such as John Cage and Merce Cunningham used the I Ching when planning their works, I felt that creating a computer program which utilises the rand function of Rust (although this is also present in other programming languages) could provide me which a way of quickly wireframing actions.
